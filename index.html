What is this?

A collection of tips, tricks, and stories for U.A.B. MD, PhD, and MD/PhD students.

Created and maintained by medical/graduate students at UAB.

Why?

Before this site existed, students had but one recourse to their questions on housing, loans, labs, mentors, courses, and other matters: GroupMe. Alas, messaging apps are a treasure trove of personal advice, upper-classmen wisdom, and hidden resources that are diluted with each incoming class of students.

This website seeks to distill all of this valuable information from GroupMe and word-of-mouth into a single resource for students to rely on (and hopefully contribute to).
